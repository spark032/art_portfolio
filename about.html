About Me

I create interactive art that invites viewers to engage and experience rather than simply observe, working across mediums from tactile sculptures to digital platforms, photography, and coding. My artistic journey began with traditional 2D and 3D work but has evolved toward digital media that can reach broader audiences.

At the heart of my practice is a desire to evoke joy and happiness while sharing personal experiences through visual storytelling. I believe in the power of visual communication and the transformative impact of participatory art experiences.
