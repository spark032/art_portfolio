Postcards from LA

Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, 2024

I created an original digital artwork using photos I took around Los Angeles, layering them with hand-drawn illustrations, custom typography, and detailed edits. As I experimented with layout and style, the piece grew into a dynamic visual journal—capturing fleeting moments, textures, and emotions from the city through a personal design lens.
