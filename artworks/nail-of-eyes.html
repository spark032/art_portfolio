Nail of Eyes

Plaster, Nails, 3D printed eyes, Yarn, Fabric, 2022
H 25" x W 16" x D 10"

This 3D sculpture is a symbolic representation of the pain that women experience from visual violence. The vibrant shades of red and the movement created by a skein of the yarn give a feeling of blood dripping down the body. The smooth texture and color of the fabric and yarn contrast with the pointy and shiny nails, highlighting the torturous experience of women that unwanted eyes viewing their bodies can cause. The 3D-printed eyes are stuck between the broken parts of the body with the web of nails to show the inescapable quality of visual violence. When positioned differently, the sculpture gives the viewers quite different feelings, especially due to the different flows and movements of the yarns.
