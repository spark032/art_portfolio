Ego

3D Sculpture, Mirror, Wood, PVC pipe, 2022
H 72" x W 15" x D 15"

This piece represents my will not to be fixated to one image but to continuously change. Rather than a mirror stuck onto the wall, which is non-adjustable, I created rotatable mirror stands to reflect myself from various angles. The performance of rotating the stands expresses my desire to become a dynamic, ever-transforming person, going against the prejudices that limit my thoughts and actions. Moreover, the clean mirror sheets show reality without distortion, helping the realization of self. The space between the stands invites the viewers to actually interact with the mirrors by moving them around and experience multi-perspectives on themselves.
